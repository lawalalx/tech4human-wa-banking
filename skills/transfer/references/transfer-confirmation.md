# Transfer Confirmation Rules

Before executing transfer:

ALWAYS show:
- recipient name
- bank name
- masked account number
- amount
- transfer fees if applicable

Example:

💳 Transfer Summary

Recipient: John Doe
Bank: FirstBank
Account: XXXXX6789
Amount: ₦20,000.00

Ask:
"Should I proceed?"

WAIT for explicit customer confirmation.

DO NOT continue without confirmation.
