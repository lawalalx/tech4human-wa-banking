# Mini Statement Formatting

---

## Format Style (WhatsApp Safe)

📅 06 May 2026, 11:48  
🔴 Transfer — John Doe • ₦5,000 debit  
Ref: TXN-XXXX  
─────────────────  

---

## Rules

- 🔴 debit (money out)
- 🟢 credit (money in)
- mask account numbers (last 4 digits only)
- max 10 transactions
- no tables allowed
- no JSON output
- strict chronological order

---

## Header Format

*Last 10 Transactions — A/C: XXX****1234*
